FixItGarage is a free (GPL-3.0) local-first vehicle maintenance tracker designed for privacy and GrapheneOS.

Track unlimited vehicles. Setup wizard for DIY, shop, or both. Log shop and DIY services (with quick templates and optional notes), fuel economy (MPG or L/100km), oil level checks every 3 months, tires (rotation diagrams, optional spare, purchases, tread depth), filters and oil part numbers with smart reminders, brakes, battery, wipers (driver/passenger/rear), costs, notes, and issue photos.

Reminders: date and mileage; date alarms re-register after reboot. Units: imperial or metric. Languages: system default or English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Korean, Simplified Chinese. Optional OpenDyslexic font for easier reading.

Backup: CSV export, full JSON backup, share to Proton Drive / Google Drive / Dropbox / OneDrive, or upload via Nextcloud/ownCloud/WebDAV. No account required. Core features do not need Google Play Services.

Send feedback via GitHub Issues from Settings.
