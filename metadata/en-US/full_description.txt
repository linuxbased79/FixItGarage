FixItGarage is a free (GPL-3.0) local-first vehicle maintenance tracker designed for privacy and GrapheneOS.

Track unlimited vehicles. Setup wizard for DIY, shop, or both. Log shop and DIY services (quick templates, notes), fuel economy (MPG or L/100km), oil level checks every 3 months, tires (rotation diagrams, optional spare, purchases, tread depth), filters and oil part numbers with smart reminders, brakes, battery, wipers, costs, notes, and issue photos.

Receipts: on-device OCR (no Google Play Services required) plus optional external OCR apps. Tread: coin-gauge camera assist with on-device computer vision estimate — always confirm before saving.

Reminders: date and mileage; date alarms re-register after reboot. Units: imperial or metric. Languages: system default or English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Korean, Simplified Chinese. Optional OpenDyslexic font.

Backup: CSV export, full JSON backup, share to Proton Drive / Google Drive / Dropbox / OneDrive, or upload via Nextcloud/ownCloud/WebDAV. No account required. Core features do not need Google Play Services.

Send feedback via GitHub Issues from Settings.
