Your garage log.

Free, open-source vehicle maintenance tracker for Android. Local-first, GrapheneOS-friendly, no account required.

Download Support development Send feedback

What it does

Built for DIY and shop users who want history, reminders, and costs without a cloud account.

Unlimited vehicles

Wizard for DIY, shop, or both. Per-car history and dashboards.

Services & MPG

Templates, notes, fuel economy (MPG or L/100km), shop + DIY.

Tires & tread

Rotation diagrams, spare option, coin-gauge CV assist, purchases.

On-device OCR

Receipt scan without Google Play Services. External OCR fallback.

Smart reminders

Date & mileage; boot-resilient local alarms. No push vendor.

Backup your way

CSV/JSON, share to Drive/Proton/Dropbox/OneDrive, or WebDAV.
